Direct Access to Candidates

Ever think about how recruiting firms talk to so many candidates that they never develop real relationships with them? At True Talent we fix that problem. We befriend our candidates so we can truly understand who they are as a person as well as their skill in their field. This allows us to better match your company's culture and needs to the best fit candidate.

In Field Recruiters

In most recruiting firms, recruiters have never held the job title that they recruit for and thus, they are largely unqualified to assess candidates from a skills perspective. True Talent combats this by interviewing candidates with interviewers who have worked in the field and therefore are able to engage in intellectual conversations about the industry, and can tell when a candidate truly knows what they're talking about.
